Hello!

My name is Maria Timmerholm! I’m 33 years old and live in Enköping, Sweden. I have a Master’s degree in Psychology and will soon have a Bachelor’s degree in Digital Design.

My interests lie in UX/UI and also Graphic design. I am good at web development even though I have a long journey ahead of me before I can say that I am brilliant. If I also get to be creative - it will end up in a user-friendly and appealing outcome.

I’m always open to bold, thoughtful ideas and collaborative opportunities. If you’re looking for someone who brings both heart and logic to the table - I’d love to connect!
