My name is Maria Timmerholm, I’m 33 years old and live in Enköping, Sweden. Year 2018 I have received my Master in Psychology from Uppsala University and will soon receive my Bachelor in Digital Design from Kristianstad University.

My interests lie in UX/UI, art direction, and Graphic design across digital envoronments and on printed matter. I am good at web development even though I have a long journey ahead of me before I can say that I am brilliant. A part of what I want to do is to make homepages that don’t make users think and question where to find a button or a specific tab. If I also get to be creative - it will end up in a user-friendly and appealing outcome.
