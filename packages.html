Packages and Prints

My huge love for Nordic minimalism is showing in a lot of my own projects.

Create a fictitious company that manufactures home furnishings. Design graphical material for the company, such as graphic elements, typography, color scheme, business card and a package for one of the comppany's products.

For this assignment, I choes to design a packaging for a porcelain plate.

Timmerholm AB is a company founded by Timmerholm family in 1996. The company is located in Sweden and sells porcelain, glass, and other types of home furnishings. Products are inspired by the Scandinavian lifestyle. Most products are either made of glass or ceramic.

The target group is adults who like stylish interior design.
